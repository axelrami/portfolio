ABOUT ME

My academic career begins at the National Preparatory School number 2 "Erasmo Castellanos Quinto" of the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) in area 1 of Physical-Mathematical Sciences and Engineering. The career chosen for the degree was Engineering in Interactive Technology and Digital Animation (ITIAD) at the Universidad del Valle de México Campus Sur.

My first formal job was QA Tester for about a year and a half for iCrossing, a digital advertising agency. My main responsibilities were to test the websites (UNICEF, Bachoco, Canon-Business) from the review of the content to the superficial critical points of the responsive design and manual tests of success-error of mobile applications. Later I was assigned more responsibilities such as content management, donation forms testing, a more in-depth review of responsive design, small front-back integrations with WordPress, wireframe design and 100% responsive site prototypes, documentation and training management and maintenance of CMS, interactive infographics programming.

Immediately after the completion of my first job I started working as QA Tester at MRM-McCann, another digital agency, having as main responsibility the review and documentation of tests of the main site of Chevrolet Mexico. Additionally, I also serve as support of the web development department for the creation of iframes and landings pages based on HTML5, review and report code-level bugs of the developments created, monthly documentation of the maintenance of the site, I am in charge of the training of the new workers in the QA department.
